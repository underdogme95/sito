# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Do not harass, demean, or discriminate against anyone.
- Keep discussions on-topic and polite.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainer directly.
